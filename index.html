About Me

Why you need a website?

DID YOU KNOW? Mobile users account for more than half (~53%) of all web traffic! If your website is not optimized for smartphones, you're missing out on the majority of clients! The *bounce rate* for an average website can be [as high as **50-75%**]. This means that for every 4 people who visit, 2 or 3 will *immediately exit* without engaging with your content. But it doesn't have to be this way!

This is where I come in: my job is to captivate your audience with a sleek, user-friendly website that showcases the best your business has to offer. When it comes to growing your business online, you don't have time for a lengthy courtship -- we're aiming for love at first sight.
